About Us
At Vital Essence, we are dedicated to bringing the finest beauty and wellness products from the USA to Sri Lanka. Our journey began with a passion for enhancing lifestyles by providing access to premium products that promote self-care and well-being.
We specialize in importing high-quality beauty and wellness brands, carefully selected to meet the needs and preferences of our discerning customers in Sri Lanka. Each product in our portfolio reflects our commitment to quality, safety, and efficacy, ensuring that our customers receive only the best.
Through our strategic partnerships and a deep understanding of the Sri Lankan market, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the beauty and wellness industry. We strive to offer products that not only meet international standards but also cater to local tastes and preferences.
History
Founded in 2020 by a civil engineer with a vision for creating a side business, our journey began by importing a single hair regrowth product from the USA. Driven by curiosity and a passion for growth, he sought new opportunities to ensure the venture's success. Amid the global challenges of COVID-19, he found a business partner with a strong background in marketing, and together they formed a partnership grounded in mutual trust and transparency, aiming to elevate the business to new heights.
The commitment and honesty of both partners quickly led to the success of their initial product in the market. This encouraged them to establish a company focused on importing premium beauty and wellness products, mainly from the USA, to ensure they offer only the best to their customers. Today, our company, Vital Essence, proudly imports a range of reputable brands, each chosen to enhance our customers' lives.
